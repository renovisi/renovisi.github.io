## Revision Policy

Revisions refer to modifications to existing content or design components within the agreed Project Specification.
Requests for major redesigns, additional pages, or new functionality are considered **Change Requests** and may incur additional charges.
Revision limits by package:

  * **Starter:** 1 revision
  * **Advance:** 3 revisions
  * **Premium:** Unlimited within reason
Revisions must be requested during the development phase, before project acceptance.
Additional revisions beyond package limits are billed at the Developer’s standard hourly rate (**RM150/hour** suggested).

---

## Maintenance Policy

Maintenance hours are included per package:
  * Starter: 5 hours
  * Advance: 10 hours
  * Premium: Custom / as agreed

Maintenance covers:
  * Minor content updates
  * Plugin or theme updates
  * Bug fixes and small adjustments
  * Performance optimizations

Maintenance does **not** include:
  * New page creation
  * Full redesigns
  * Custom feature development

Maintenance hours must be used within **12 months** of project release.
Unused hours do **not** carry forward and are non-refundable.
Additional maintenance hours beyond package limit will be billed at **RM150/hour** suggested.

## Payment Terms

1. **Deposit:** 50% of total package fee required to start the project.
2. **Build Phase Payment:** 50% before commencement of the main development phase.
3. **Final Payment:** Remaining balance due within 10 days of Acceptance Test Period completion.
4. All fees are **exclusive of GST**.
5. Payments must be made in full without deductions.
6. Late payments may result in suspension of the project, disabling of hosted websites, or termination of the Agreement.

---

## Additional Notes

* Any requests beyond the agreed scope are treated as **Change Requests** and billed separately.
* All projects include a **10-day Acceptance Test Period** for client review and confirmation.
* Domain registration, hosting setup, and SSL certificates are included in package fees but billed separately if custom or premium services are requested.
* All packages include a **Project Release** upon acceptance, transferring ownership of the website to the Client.
* Premium Package pricing is **customized** based on project scope, features, and complexity.
