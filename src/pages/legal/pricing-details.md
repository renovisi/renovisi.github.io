## Package Details

### 1. Starter Package – RM999

Ideal for businesses without an existing website.

**Features:**
- 1-page website (Landing page / One-page business site)
- Basic SEO setup (meta tags, titles)
- Contact form integrated with email
- .com domain registration + basic shared hosting
- **Revisions:** 1 revision  
- **Maintenance:** 5 hours within 12 months

**Use Case:**  
Perfect for businesses wanting a simple, affordable web presence.

---

### 2. Advance Package – RM2,999

For growing businesses that need more content and control.

**Features:**
- Up to 8 pages  
- SEO-friendly page structure  
- **Basic Text-Only CMS** (edit text & replace images only)  
- .com domain registration + standard shared hosting  
- **Revisions:** 3 revisions  
- **Maintenance:** 10 hours within 12 months  

**Use Case:**  
Ideal for businesses planning to update content independently without design changes.

---

### 3. Premium Package – Custom Pricing

For businesses requiring advanced features and scalability.

**Features:**
- Unlimited pages  
- Optimized SEO & performance  
- **Advanced CMS** with dynamic content, blog, structured data, or integrations  
- .com domain registration + private hosting  
- **Revisions:** Multiple revisions (within reasonable scope)  
- **Maintenance:** Custom hours  

**Use Case:**  
Suitable for businesses requiring advanced functionality and long-term support.

## CMS Explanation

### 1. Basic Text-Only CMS (Advance Package)
A simple CMS enabling:
- Edit text on existing pages  
- Update headings, descriptions, contacts  
- Replace existing images  

**Limitations:**
- No new pages  
- No layout or design changes  
- No blog/article publishing  
- No custom features  

### 2. Advanced CMS (Premium Package)
Offers full content management:
- Blog/articles  
- Dynamic content  
- Product/services management  
- Custom modules  
- Integrations (API, booking, e-commerce)  

---

## Revision Policy

Revisions refer to modifications to existing content or design within the agreed Project Specification.

**Revision Limits:**
- **Starter:** 1 revision  
- **Advance:** 3 revisions  
- **Premium:** Unlimited (within reasonable scope)

**Notes:**
- Major redesigns, new pages, or new features are considered **Change Requests** and billed separately.
- Additional revisions: **RM150/hour**.

---

## Maintenance Policy

Maintenance ensures the website remains secure, updated, and functional.

### Included Maintenance Tasks
- Minor content updates  
- Image replacement (within existing layout)  
- Bug fixes and small layout adjustments  
- Performance improvements  
- Security updates, CMS/plugin updates  
- Hosting/domain troubleshooting  
- Backups (if applicable)

### Not Included
- New pages  
- New features or integrations  
- Full redesign or rebranding  
- Blog/article writing  
- E-commerce management  
- Adding new sections requiring design work  

### Package Maintenance Hours
- Starter: **5 hours (12 months)**  
- Advance: **10 hours (12 months)**  
- Premium: **Custom**  

Unused hours do not roll over.  
Additional maintenance is billed at **RM150/hour**.

## Payment Terms

1. **Deposit:** 50% required to start the project.  
2. **Build Phase Payment:** 50% before main development begins.  
3. **Final Payment:** Due within 10 days after Acceptance Test Period.  
4. All fees are **exclusive of GST**.  
5. Late payment may result in suspension or termination.  
6. All payments must be made in full with no deductions.

---

## Additional Notes
- Any requests beyond the agreed scope are **Change Requests**.  
- All projects include a **10-day Acceptance Test Period**.  
- Domain, hosting, and SSL are included unless custom services are required.  
- Project Release transfers ownership to the Client upon acceptance.  
- Premium Package pricing is fully custom based on project complexity.
